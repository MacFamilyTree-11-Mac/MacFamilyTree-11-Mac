## What Is MacFamilyTree 11 for Mac

macfamilytree for mac is a comprehensive genealogy program that Synium Software designed specifically for the
Mac. Rather than treating your family history as a flat list of names and dates, it turns the people, events
and sources you enter into an explorable world of interactive charts, 3D views, maps and richly formatted
reports. Everything you record is your own research, stored locally on your Mac unless you decide to sync it
with CloudTree or publish it as a website.

## Before You Download

A few things worth knowing before you download macfamilytree for mac, so it fits how you actually plan to work.

- It is a full desktop app for macOS, sold as a one-time purchase rather than tiered editions.
- Your data lives locally by default; iCloud storage through CloudTree is optional and encrypted.
- GEDCOM import and export let you move a tree in and out of other genealogy programs freely.
- FamilySearch integration and web research help grow a tree, but you supply and verify the facts.
- MobileFamilyTree 11 for iPhone and iPad is a separate companion purchase, not required for the Mac app.

## Everyday Workflow

In daily use, macfamilytree 11 mac download follows a simple loop: add a person, attach the sources that back
them up, and watch the Interactive Tree redraw itself. When you find records online, FamilySearch and web
research feed straight into your tree, and the Plausibility report flags dates or relationships that do not add
up. When you want to see patterns, switch to the 3D Globe, Timeline, Fan chart or Sociogram. When you want to
share, publish a free family tree website or assemble a printable family book.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I move an existing tree into MacFamilyTree?**
Use GEDCOM import. macfamilytree download mac reads GEDCOM files from other genealogy programs, and it can
export your tree back to GEDCOM whenever you want.

**Is my genealogy data kept private?**
Yes. genealogy software mac like this keeps everything on your Mac by default; nothing is uploaded unless you
turn on CloudTree sync or publish a website yourself.

**Can I research online records from inside the app?**
Yes. macfamilytree 11 mac integrates FamilySearch with billions of genealogical entries and links out to
popular genealogy sites for web research.

**What kinds of reports and charts can it make?**
macfamilytree for mac generates person, family and kinship reports, a narrative report, place and event lists,
plus fan, relationship and hourglass charts, a genogram and a Timeline.

**Can I publish or print my family history?**
Yes. You can create and host a free family tree website through MacFamilyTree.com and assemble printable family
books, and export individual charts and reports to various formats.

**Does it work with an iPhone or iPad?**
It can. macfamilytree 11 mac download pairs with MobileFamilyTree 11 (sold separately) and CloudTree real-time
sync so your research continues on the go.

**What is new in version 11?**
This release adds a completely new Places Management system, a Sociogram diagram, AI photo colorization and
repair, an improved interface with a new Welcome Window, and a new backup feature for your data.

**Do I need to be an experienced genealogist to use it?**
No. A family tree app mac like MacFamilyTree pairs a clear, guided interface for beginners with deep tools —
GEDCOM exchange, the full reporting suite and 3D visualizations — for advanced researchers.
